ABOUT US

OUR JOURNEY

Welcome to Let’s Ladakh – a dedicated travel initiative by Safari Sutra Holidays.

Established in January 2024 and headquartered in Mumbai, we are a team of passionate travel enthusiasts committed to unveiling the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Ladakh – the Land of High Passes.

Join us on an unforgettable journey to explore the true essence of this Himalayan paradise!

OUR VISION

At Let’s Ladakh, we redefine travel by offering more than just sightseeing—we create transformative journeys that leave lasting impressions. Our vision is to revolutionize how travelers experience the majestic Himalayas, ensuring every trip is immersive, meaningful, and respectful of local communities and the environment.

Our mission? To curate authentic, sustainable, and deeply connected travel experiences that bring you closer to the soul of Ladakh. 🌿🏔✨

What Makes Let’s Ladakh Unique?

As part of Safari Sutra Holidays Private Limited, Let’s Ladakh benefits from our parent company’s expertise in crafting tailored travel experiences across India.

What Sets Us Apart?

🌍 Deep Local Connections
Our team includes local Ladakhi experts who provide insider knowledge and authentic cultural insights, ensuring a truly immersive experience.

♻️ Responsible Tourism
We are committed to minimizing environmental impacts through careful waste management, supporting local economies, and preserving Ladakh's delicate ecological balance.

🎒 Customized Experiences
Whether you're an adventure enthusiast seeking high-altitude treks, a photography buff chasing perfect shots of moonlike landscapes, or a spiritual seeker exploring ancient monasteries, we craft journeys tailored to your interests.

🛡️ Safety & Comfort
We understand the unique challenges of high-altitude travel and prioritize your well-being with comprehensive acclimatization plans and 24/7 support.
